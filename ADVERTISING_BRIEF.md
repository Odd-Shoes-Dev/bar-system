# 📋 Project Brief — Salon Management System
### For: Advertising Video Production / Client Sharing
---

## 1. Overview

**Product Name:** Blue Ox — Salon Management System
**Product Type:** Web-based business management platform for salons and beauty businesses
**Tagline (suggested):** *Run your salon smarter. From the front desk to the back office — all in one place.*

This is a complete digital solution built specifically for salons, nail care studios, spas, and beauty businesses. It replaces paper records, manual calculations, and scattered tools with one clean, easy-to-use system accessible from any device — phone, tablet, or computer.

---

## 2. Who Is This For?

| Audience | How They Use It |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Salon Owners** | Oversee the entire business — sales, staff, reports, and settings |
| **Managers** | Handle daily operations, track performance, manage stock |
| **Stylists / Technicians** | Get assigned to clients, track their services |
| **Cashiers / Receptionists** | Process payments, check in clients, manage walk-ins |

---

## 3. Core Features (For the Video)

These are the key features to highlight. Each one solves a real problem for salon owners.

---

### 💳 Point of Sale (POS)
- Quickly add services to a client's visit
- Select which staff member performed each service
- Apply discounts and loyalty point redemptions
- Accept multiple payment methods: **Cash, MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money**
- Print or send receipts digitally
- Fast, simple checkout — takes less than a minute

> 🎬 *Video idea: Show a receptionist quickly checking out a happy client on a tablet at the front desk*

---

### 👥 Client Management
- Store every client's name, phone, and visit history
- Track how much each client has spent over time
- See which services they love
- Add notes about client preferences
- **Birthday alerts** — automatically know when a client has a birthday and send them a special discount

> 🎬 *Video idea: Receptionist pulls up a returning client and greets them by name — "Welcome back, Sarah! Happy Birthday! You have a 15% discount today."*

---

### 🎁 Loyalty & Rewards Program
- Clients earn points automatically with every visit
- Points can be redeemed for discounts on future visits
- Encourages clients to keep coming back
- Fully automatic — no manual tracking needed

---

### 📅 Referral System
- Track how new clients found the salon (e.g., social media, walk-in, referred by a friend)
- Reward clients who refer others
- Understand which marketing channels bring the most business

---

### 💼 Staff Management
- Add staff profiles (stylists, technicians, managers)
- Track which staff member served each client
- Monitor individual performance through reports
- Control who has access to what — different roles for owners, managers, and cashiers

---

### 📦 Inventory & Stock Tracking
- Keep track of products and supplies used in the salon
- Get alerts before items run out
- Organize stock by category (hair products, nail supplies, skincare, etc.)
- Know exactly what you have without counting manually

---

### 💰 Expense Tracking
- Record salon expenses (rent, supplies, utilities, salaries)
- Categorize spending to understand where money goes
- Compare expenses vs. revenue to know your profitability

---

### 📊 Reports & Analytics
- Daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports
- Revenue breakdown by service category
- Best-performing staff members
- Most popular services
- Visual charts and graphs — easy to understand at a glance
- Export and review from anywhere

> 🎬 *Video idea: Salon owner sitting with a coffee, checking the day's revenue on their phone from home*

---

### ⚙️ Salon Settings & Branding
- Each salon has its own branded experience — custom logo and brand colors
- Customize SMS templates sent to clients
- Configure loyalty point rates
- Set up birthday discount percentages

---

## 4. Key Selling Points (The "Why Choose This")

1. **Works on any device** — phone, tablet, or desktop, anywhere with internet
2. **No training required** — clean, simple interface anyone can learn in minutes
3. **Everything in one place** — no more juggling spreadsheets, notebooks, and separate apps
4. **Real-time visibility** — owners see what's happening in the salon even when not physically there
5. **Client retention tools** — loyalty points, birthday rewards, and referral tracking keep clients coming back
6. **Multi-location ready** — one platform can manage multiple salon branches
7. **Secure** — each salon's data is fully private and isolated

---

## 5. The Problem This Solves

Many salons today still use:
- ❌ Paper appointment books that get lost or damaged
- ❌ Manual cash counting with no record of who paid what
- ❌ WhatsApp groups to track client info
- ❌ Guesswork to know which services make the most money
- ❌ No system to reward loyal clients

**Blue Ox replaces all of that** with one affordable, professional system that works from day one.
